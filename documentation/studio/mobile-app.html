Evothings Client

The Evothings Client app is the companion for the Evothings Workbench. This is a ready-to-use app, that will get you started instantly with building IoT-enabled mobile applications.

Evothings Client provides:

  • Automatic connection to Evothings Workbench.
  • Pre-packaged native libraries that extends JavaScript with advanced networking and connectivity for IoT.
  • Support for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).
  • Automatic reload when you edit code on your desktop/laptop machine.
  • Full functionality of HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, as provided by the platform of your phone or tablet's operating system.
  • Full compatibility with Apache Cordova plugins.
  • Source code provided to assist when building your production apps.

Evothings Client is available on the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store. Four further details, please refer to the Evothings download page.
